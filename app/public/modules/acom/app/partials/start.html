Starting a Collection

Collections may be requested by any unit of Willamette University that:

  • produces scholarly content
  • has a defined leader
  • has long-term stability
  • and can assume responsibility for setting community policies

The unit must assign a collection coordinator to work with Academic Commons staff.

The Academic Commons Coordinator will meet with the department or its assigned collection coordinator to define:

  • The purpose of the collection
  • Collection information: name, description, logo
  • Names and email addresses of submitters
  • Names and email addresses of people in workflow roles
  • The intended audience
  • Intellectual property ownership
  • The types of materials or publications (published or unpublished articles, images, datasets)
  • File formats (PDF, JPEG, TIFF)
  • Disk space requirements

Once an initial review is completed, the Academic Commons Coordinator, a liaison librarian, or other staff will then work with the department to create a collection that meets its requirements. If the Academic Commons is not the appropriate location for the material, staff will attempt to recommend an alternate solution.

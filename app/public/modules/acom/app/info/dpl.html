Digital Production Lab

The Digital Production Lab provides services geared toward academic projects.

The Digital Production Lab is managed by the Hatfield Library Systems Division and provides students with employment opportunities that use technology for a variety of academic projects. Past projects include:

  • Digitizing historical image collections from the University Archives.
  • Automated text transcription of yearbooks.
  • Capturing newspaper layout coordinates for online display.
  • Writing item descriptions and conducting background research.
  • Creating exhibits for touch screen kiosks and the web.

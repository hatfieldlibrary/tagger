Submit Research

The Academic Commons is enhanced by community contributions from faculty, students and staff.

Student Theses and Projects

Student works are an essential part of the Academic Commons, including senior theses, independent research, grant proposals, and multimedia projects. See the instructions below for information on how to submit your student project.

Student Submissions

Students can directly submit work to these collections:

  • Senior Theses collection
  • Liberal Arts Research Collaborative (LARC)
  • Science Collaborative Research Program (SCRP)

For help, see this Tutorial or contact John Repplinger (jrepplin@willamette.edu or 503-370-6525).

Faculty and Academic Departments

Faculty and academic departments can request an Academic Commons collection. Collections can be for departmental use only or for general distribution. When a collection is created for your department we will set the access level. Permission can be limited to a single individual, a group of individuals or to Willamette University.

Contact one of our staff if you need help submitting to your collection.

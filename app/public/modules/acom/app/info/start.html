Collection Criteria

Collections can be requested by any unit of Willamette University that:

  • Produces content related to the University's academic mission
  • Is a permanent department or organization
  • Has a point person who assumes responsibility for collections

The Academic Commons coordinator will meet with the department or its designated collection coordinator to discuss:

  • The purpose of the collection
  • The intended audience
  • Intellectual property issues
  • The types of materials or publications (published or unpublished articles, images, datasets)
  • File formats (PDF, JPEG, TIFF)
  • Disk space requirements

Once the review is complete, the Academic Commons coordinator or your liaison librarian will work with your department to create a collection that meets your needs.

To request a collection, contact Michael Spalti or Bill Kelm.

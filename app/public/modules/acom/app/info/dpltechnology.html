Digital Production Lab Technology

Equipment

The following equipment is used in the Digital Production Lab.

Epson Expression 10000XL Scanner
Scanning of archival material including photographs, texts, slides and glass plate negatives. Accomodates up to 12.2" x 17.2"
Epson Stylus Photo R1900 Ink Jet Printer
Printing reproductions of materials, signage up to 13" wide x 44".
Plustek OpticBook 4800
Book edge scanner offering higher speed scanning of bound matierial 8 1/2 x 11.
Fujitsu fi-4340C Flatbed Scanner
Bulk scanning of text materials where sheet feeder use is acceptable.
Atiz BookDrive Pro
Book scanning, used in conjucnction with ABBYY Recognition Server and eXistdb.
Roxio easy VHS to DVD
VCR to Mac cable and software for VHS to digital conversion.
Toshiba DVR620 DVD/VHS Recorder
VHS DVD recorder for converting VHS to directly to DVD.

20" Sony Trinitron model KV-20FV12
TV for VHS playback, attached to above recorder for transfer to DVD.

SmartDisk USB Floppy Drive - Color Kit Edition
External Floppy Drive.
Software

The Academic Commons uses a variety of Open Source and Commercial software, including:

Presentation:

  • Web Servers: Apache / Tomcat / Nodejs
  • CONTENTdm
  • DSpace
  • eXistdb
  • Custom interfaces built with Google Web Toolkit and AngularJS

Workflow:

  • ABBYY Recognition Server
  • Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Acrobat
  • ffmpeg
  • MacX DVD Ripper Pro
